答案解析

  1. a. Yes, I always write some to-do lists to keep my life organized.

    b. No, I don’t. Sometimes, I’ll make mental lists. I can remember what I need to do.

  2. a. ‌ Yes, I can. No matter what kind of task it is, if I’m supposed to finish it in time, I will finish it as scheduled.

    b. ‌ No, I can’t. Some tasks are just too difficult and time-consuming. It’s just impossible for me to finish all of them in time especially when they start piling up.

  3. The man portrayed in this cartoon is a typical procrastinator. He might never get that task done.

Task 3

答案解析

  1. \1. How can we manage our time effectively? First, we should make a list of all the tasks we need to accomplish. Then, we need to focus on our most productive time of day and tackle them in priority order. We should also observe, contemplate and create in order to find ways to save time.
  2. \2. Do you have any symptoms of procrastination? What are they? a. ‌ Yes, I have. Whenever I’m assigned some boring or difficult task, I would avoid doing it until several hours before the deadline. b. No, I’m not a procrastinator. I prefer completing all the tasks before the schedule.
  3. \3. Why do people procrastinate? I believe that people procrastinate because they are either physically tired, or mentally stressed, or not motivated. If they are passionate about what they are doing, they won’t procrastinate
